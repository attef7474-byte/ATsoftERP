# Session 2: Inventory Operations

> Training session for inventory team — 2 hours

## Objectives

By the end of this session, you will be able to:
- View warehouses and locations
- Browse products and categories
- Check inventory balances
- View movement history
- Understand inventory counts

## Warehouses

1. Navigate to **Warehouses** in the sidebar
2. View the list of warehouses
3. Each warehouse has a name, code, and status

## Locations

1. Navigate to **Locations** under Inventory
2. Each location belongs to a warehouse
3. Locations represent aisles, racks, or bins

## Products

1. Navigate to **Products**
2. View product list with code, name, category, unit
3. Click a product to see details

## Product Categories

1. Navigate to **Product Categories**
2. Categories are organized in a tree
3. Use the Tree view to see parent-child relationships

## Inventory Balances

1. Navigate to **Balances**
2. View stock quantities per product and location
3. The summary shows total levels

## Inventory Movements

1. Navigate to **Movements**
2. View stock in/out transactions
3. Filter by product or date

## Inventory Counts

1. Navigate to **Counts**
2. View physical count plans
3. Counts reconcile system balances with physical stock

## Practical Exercise

1. Find a product in the product list
2. Check its balance in a specific warehouse
3. View recent movements for that product
4. Try the search (F9) to find a warehouse

## Summary

You now know how to:
- Navigate inventory modules
- Check stock levels
- View movements
- Understand counts
