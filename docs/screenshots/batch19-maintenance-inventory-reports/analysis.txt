Batch 19 — Maintenance and Inventory Reports
=============================================
Goal: Build real maintenance and inventory report pages with backend API,
      server-side aggregation, F9 filters, summary cards, CSV export,
      browser print, and Action Bar integration.

- lib/i18n/                 — reports namespace (100+ keys en + ar),
                             navigation keys for sidebar labels
